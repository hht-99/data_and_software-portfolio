#### Bullet Point Summary

##### Profitability:
- 300 out of 1545 Products either make a loss or breakeven
- The “Technology” category is the most profitable.
- Sub-category “Tables” loses the most money — consider price changes, discount reduction or removal.
- Discounts above 20% correlate with increasingly negative profit margins.
- Region “West” performs best, Region “Central” underperforms.
- Top 10 customers contribute nearly 18% of all profit.
- Recommendation: reduce deep discounting in subcategory “Binders”, lowest margin at -20%.
